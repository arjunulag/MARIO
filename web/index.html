of a learning agent.

A custom side-scrolling platformer built straight in your browser, paired with a swappable ghost replay system. Today it ships with a hand-tuned sample run. Soon, the ghost will be the trajectory of our reinforcement learning model — trained from scratch on NumPy.

▶ Start Playing How it works

What makes this different

Most Mario clones are nostalgia projects. This one is a benchmark — a place where humans and a learning agent share the same level, the same physics, and the same finish line.

⚙

Pure-browser engine

HTML5 canvas, deterministic physics, tile-based level. No emulators, no plugins — just open the page and play.

👻

Shadow ghost racing

A translucent ghost runs the level beside you in real time. Beat it to the flag — or watch and study its lines.

🔬

Swappable trajectory

The ghost loads from a JSON file. When the RL model finishes training, drop in its trajectory and the ghost becomes the agent.

📄

NumPy-only stack

Backed by a from-scratch ML library: MLPs, manual backprop, Adam, and a DQN agent — all written without PyTorch or TensorFlow.

⚙

Reproducible runs

Same level, same seed, same physics. Compare runs side by side — your best vs. the agent's best.

⏱

Personal best timer

Your fastest clear is saved in the browser and stays on the play page when you come back.

🏆

Race-mode HUD

Live timer, coin counter, and a position differential telling you exactly how far ahead or behind the ghost you are.

How to play

Two minutes of reading, a lifetime of pit-falling. Here's everything you need.

1

Open the play page

Click Start Playing above. The level loads instantly — no install, no signup. The shadow ghost is enabled by default; toggle it off any time.

2

Beat the level

Run right, jump over pits, stomp Goombas, collect coins, and reach the flag at the end of the stage. The clock starts on your first input.

3

Race the ghost

The shadow ghost runs the same level using the same physics. The HUD shows your live differential — green means you're ahead, red means catch up.

4

Try, fail, retry

Press R at any time to restart instantly. Falling in a pit or getting stomped resets the run. The ghost resets with you.

About the agent

Today: a scripted ghost. Soon: a real learning agent.

⏱

Right now

The ghost is a deterministic scripted run baked into data/ghost_sample.json. It uses the same physics engine you do, so the race is fair to the frame.

🧠

Soon

Once the DQN agent finishes training on the Mario environment, we'll dump its best episode trajectory into the same JSON format. The ghost stays the same — the brain behind it changes.
